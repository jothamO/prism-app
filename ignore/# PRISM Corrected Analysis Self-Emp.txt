## PRISM IMPROVEMENT SUMMARY

### **What This Real Scenario Teaches PRISM:**

1. **Context is EVERYTHING**
   - Large transfer ‚â† Business sale
   - Could be: Capital, loan, gift, reimbursement
   - MUST ask user for context

2. **Startup founders have unique profiles**
   - Pre-revenue with high expenses
   - Support from investors/family (not sales)
   - Usually have VAT refunds, not payables

3. **Mixed accounts are normal**
   - Same account for business + personal
   - Need user help to classify EVERY ambiguous transaction
   - Pattern learning over time

4. **Expense classification needs purpose**
   - "What did you use this for?" is critical
   - Same expense (electricity) can be personal or business
   - Documentation requirements vary

5. **VAT registration is strategic**
   - Don't assume user is registered
   - Explain voluntary registration benefits
   - Compare trade-offs (simplicity vs. refunds)
